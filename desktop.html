IDM-211: INTERFACE DESIGN I

None Such Farm & Market is a locally owned business with a tight knit community. They strive on forming realtionships with their customers and depend on their loyalty. I wanted to ensure that the small town farm feel wasn't lost in my re-design, so I focused on incorporating images that I felt encompased their original aesthetic. I did, however, also want to incorporate odern design elements in my design to give the site a professional feel to is as well. This can be seen through the modern design of image carosels as well as the consistent cohesion across the site. My re-design of the None Such Farm & Market website maintains their farm town image, but elevates thier professionalism.
