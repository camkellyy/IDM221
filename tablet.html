CONTACT DESKTOP

Musion is a concept that I came up with as a way to bring people together through music. User's take a short survery about different artists, musics, and albums that they like, and they are given three music tastes with various percentages that line up with their answers. They can then see other people in their local area, by zip code, who have overlapping music tastes with them. In addition , there are concert dates listed that line up with the user's location and music taste. This website is aimed at college students and recent graduates, so I wanted to have a modern yet minimalistic dseign approach that would appeal to that crowd.
